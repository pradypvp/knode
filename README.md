Knode - A Skill Exchange Platform

Transforming campus learning through peer-to-peer knowledge sharing.

📌 Overview

Knode is a skill-exchange platform designed to bridge the gap between students seeking academic help and peers who possess the required expertise. It creates a verified knowledge-sharing ecosystem where students can collaborate, earn credibility, and receive timely assistance without relying solely on faculty availability.

🚀 Problem Statement

Large classroom environments often create delays in receiving academic support:

Students wait long periods for debugging help.
Faculty cannot efficiently provide one-on-one assistance to everyone.
Valuable peer knowledge remains unstructured and underutilized.

Knode addresses these challenges by enabling instant peer-to-peer academic support.
